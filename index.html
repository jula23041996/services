Our services

Green Blue Edge is a leading provider of renewable energy project development and implementation services.

Feasibility Studies

Green Blue Edge's Feasibility Studies provide clients with a comprehensive and detailed assessment of the technical, economic, and environmental viability of renewable energy projects.

Scope Assessment

We will analyse target emissions reductions, target sustainability and environmental performance, regulatory requirements, target market, project size and scale, timeline, budget, and stakeholders to understand the client organisation's goals and requirements.

Technical Assessment

This includes conducting site assessments, evaluating the potential for renewable energy generation, and determining the feasibility of project design options, as well as grid connection, energy storage, and renewable energy system integration.

Economic Assessment

We evaluate the costs associated with project development, implementation, and operation, as well as the potential revenue streams from the sale of carbon credits and renewable energy. We also consider factors such as financing options, tax incentives, and subsidies, and we provide recommendations for optimizing the economic viability of the project.

Enviromental Assessment

We consider factors such as waste management, land use, emissions reductions, water usage, and biodiversity conservation, to issue recommendations for mitigating any potential impacts.

Feasibility Report

Once we have carried out the various assessments and associated analysis, we bring all the information together to provide a comprehensive and detailed feasibility study report. This report includes recommendations as well as an action plan for moving forward.

Verification & Certification

Certifying projects is a complex and time-consuming task. We are here to help our clients navigate the process to receive carbon credits from the right accreditation bodies.

Project assessment

We will conduct a comprehensive assessment of your project. We will evaluate its design, implementation, and monitoring plans, and provide recommendations to meet the requirements of the certification scheme.

Verification and Validation

Once the project is ready for certification, we will work with independent third-party verifiers to conduct on-site assessments and validate the emissions reductions generated by the project. We are here to ensure that all requirements are met.

Documentation preparation

We will work with you to prepare all the necessary documentation, including the project design document, monitoring and reporting plans, and any other relevant information. Accuracy and thoroughness is key.

Monitoring and reporting

We will assist you in monitoring and reporting the emissions reductions generated by the project, to gather accurate and reliable data. Our aim is that the project continues to meet the requirements of the certification scheme.

Stakeholder engagement

We understand the importance of engaging with stakeholders, including local communities and other project partners, in the certification process. Our team will work with you to develop a stakeholder engagement plan and ensure that all stakeholders are informed and involved in the project.

Carbon credit sales

Once the carbon credits have been generated, our team of experts can assist with the sale of the credits. We have extensive experience in the carbon credit market, and we can help clients to secure the best possible price for their carbon credits.
